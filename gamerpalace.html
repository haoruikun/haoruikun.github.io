GamerPalace

GamerPalace is a mobile app for a hotel dedicated to providing best living experiences for gamers. In GamerPalace, residents can not only find the best room that cater their hobbies, but also socialize with other gamers in the hotel.

Mobile UI Design (This is a small class assignment, not a complete product)

Role: Came up with product concept, conducted user research, and created the UI and Figma prototype.

Timeline: 1 week (short design challenge)

The Problem

There are many gamers who travel- they like to travel during the day, and want to play games during the night in hotels.

However, most hotels donâ€™t provide the gaming equipments gamers need.

User Research

I interviewed 5 gamers. I first asked them what do they want in the hotel for their next trip that could cater their gaming needs. Their answers varies a lot because they like to play different kinds of games. Some people want high performing computer, some want gaming consoles, and some want table and collections of board games. So I figure that we should provide different kinds of room catering different gaming needs. We can have different themes like PC themes, console themes, and board game themes.

Then I move on to the app itself, I asked them what features do they want in the hotel app. I got a lot of feedback. Besides some common basic features like browsing and booking , many of my interviewees pointed out that they wanted some kind of chatting features that can enable them to chat with other gamers in the hotel so they can play games together. I decide to incorporate this feature in the app so our customers can have a good time and even build a gamer community that could be our loyal long-term customer group.

The Goals

  1. Provide basic functions like browsing, booking, and locating the hotel.
  2. Display different room themes for different types of gamers.
  3. Include a chatting function that allows gamers in the hotel to socialize.
